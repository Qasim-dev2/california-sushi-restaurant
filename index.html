Our Story

Authentic Japanese
Craftsmanship

Since 2014, The California Sushi has been Windsor's destination for premium Japanese cuisine. We combine traditional Japanese techniques with fresh, carefully sourced ingredients to deliver an experience that honours the true art of sushi — every roll, every plate, every bite.

From our handcrafted special rolls to authentic nigiri sashimi, our chefs pour passion into every dish. Dine in, pick up, or order delivery — the California Sushi experience travels with you.
